The article outlines a three-layered approach to game optimization: profiling to identify performance bottlenecks, validation to catch problematic assets at submission rather than QA, and automation to track performance trends across builds. Rather than optimizing harder near release, successful studios proactively prevent regressions by making performance data visible to teams throughout development.
